Product Details

The Hex Bar, also known as a trap bar, is a versatile strength-training tool designed for performing exercises like deadlifts, shrugs, and farmer’s walks. Its unique hexagonal shape allows users to stand inside the bar, promoting a more natural lifting position that reduces strain on the lower back while engaging the legs, glutes, and core. Suitable for all fitness levels, the hex bar helps improve strength, power, and overall lifting technique with enhanced stability and control.
